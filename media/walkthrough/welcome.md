# Welcome to Dumb Formatter

This extension strips smart Unicode formatting and hidden characters out of your text:

- Em-dashes, en-dashes, and Unicode minus
- Smart single and double quotes
- Non-breaking spaces and other Unicode space variants
- Ellipsis, bullets, soft hyphens, zero-width spaces
- BOM, ZWJ, ZWNJ, bidi formatting marks, word joiner
- Optionally: presentation-form ligatures (off by default)

All commands work on demand without enabling anything else. The next steps offer optional automatic features - they all default to **off**.
